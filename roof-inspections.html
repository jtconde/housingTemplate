Roof Inspections — Honest Answers, Fast

Free, no-obligation inspections for any situation — routine checkups, storm damage, or insurance claims. We get on the roof and tell you exactly what we find.

What We Check on Every Inspection

A Mason inspection isn't a quick glance from the driveway. We get on the roof and look at everything — so you know exactly what you're working with.

Shingles & Surface Materials

Cracked, curling, missing, or blistering shingles. Granule loss that signals age and UV damage. We check every section of the roof surface.

Flashing & Leak Points

Chimneys, skylights, vents, and wall intersections — the most common sources of leaks. We check all flashing for rust, separation, and failed seals.

Ventilation & Decking

Ridge vents, soffit vents, and insulation levels. Plus a check for soft spots, sagging, and rot in the decking beneath your shingles.

Hail & Wind Damage

Hail damage isn't always visible from the ground. We know exactly where to look — and document everything you need to support an insurance claim.

Storm Damage & Insurance Claims

We've helped hundreds of homeowners navigate the insurance process after storm damage. We know what adjusters look for — and we document it properly.

  • Detailed photo documentation of all damage
  • Written inspection report for your claim
  • We meet your insurance adjuster on-site if needed
  • Estimates formatted to match insurance scopes
  • No claim filed? We give you an honest assessment anyway

Storm damage claims typically must be filed within one year of the event. If you've had a recent storm, don't wait.
